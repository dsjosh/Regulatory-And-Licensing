[checklist]
1 = Is the datacentre's electrical wiring colour coded to Singapore standards?
2 = Did the datacentre use large enough wire gauge suitable for the expected power draw?
3 = Are there fuses at every outlet in the datacentre?
4 = Has the datacentre exposed any wiring without proper trunking?
5 = Are high-voltage and low-voltage datacentre wiring separated?
